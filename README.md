# 🚗 Austo Customer Behavior Analysis

A business analytics project that explores customer demographics, financial profiles, and automobile purchasing behavior to generate actionable insights for improving marketing campaigns and customer experience.

---

## 📌 Business Problem

Austo Motor Company manufactures SUVs, Sedans, and Hatchbacks.

The company wants to better understand customer demand and purchasing behavior to improve marketing effectiveness and enhance customer experience.

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify trends in customer demographics, income, loan status, and vehicle preferences.

---

## 📊 Dataset

The dataset contains information about **1,581 customers** and includes demographic, financial, and automobile purchase attributes.

### Features

- Age
- Gender
- Profession
- Marital Status
- Education
- Number of Dependents
- Personal Loan
- House Loan
- Partner Working
- Salary
- Partner Salary
- Total Salary
- Vehicle Price
- Vehicle Make

## 📊 Business Insights

Key findings include:

- Most customers are young working professionals.
- Sedans are the most purchased vehicle category.
- Female customers spend more on average despite representing a smaller share of buyers.
- Personal loan holders contribute significantly to vehicle purchases.
- Household income strongly influences purchasing power.
- Partner employment has limited impact on spending behavior.

## 💼 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Target salaried professionals with personalized campaigns.
- Promote premium vehicle variants to higher-income segments.
- Design financing offers for customers requiring loans.
- Focus marketing efforts on Sedan buyers while increasing awareness for SUVs.
- Develop customer-specific promotional strategies based on demographics and purchasing behavior.
